# 07 — Help Center, Legal & Cookie Consent

Help Center berisi panduan, FAQ, status, changelog, API overview, troubleshooting, Acceptable Use, Refund, Copyright/DMCA, Service Level, TOS, Privacy, Cookies, dan Security.

Jika `NEXT_PUBLIC_HELP_URL=https://help.xystudio.my.id`, link TOS/Privacy/Cookies pada footer otomatis menuju subdomain help.

## Cookie consent

Banner menyediakan:

- Terima semua
- Tolak opsional
- Atur kategori
- Esensial selalu aktif
- Analytics hanya setelah consent
- Pengaturan ulang melalui icon cookie

Legal copy perlu ditinjau penasihat hukum sebelum peluncuran komersial lintas yurisdiksi.
